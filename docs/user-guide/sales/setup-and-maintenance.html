    Setup & Maintenance

    The Setup & Maintenance menu enlists the following sub-menus:

    [Salespersons](setup/salespersons.html) Salesperson is an employee, agent, consultant, or an independent contractor brining business to your organization.
    [Sales Teams](setup/sales-teams.html) A sales team is a group of salespersons having a common name.
    [Bonus Slabs for Salespersons](setup/bonus-slabs.html) Salespersons can be entitled to a bonus, depending upon the amount (slab) of business brought in by them.
    [Bonus Slab Details](setup/bonus-slab-details.html) Enter a slab-wise incremental or flat bonus rate (in percentage) which increases as the amount increases.
    [Bonus Slab Assignment](setup/bonus-slab-assignment.html) Assign bonus slabs to salespersons.
    [Late Fees](setup/late-fees.html) Create your custom defined late fees, which can be linked to other documents, such as payment term.
    [Payment Terms](setup/payment-terms.html) Define the terms on which you will provide credit to your parties.
    [Recurring Invoices](setup/recurring-invoices.html) Create invoices which automatically recur based on your item, product, or services.
    [Recurring Invoices Setup](setup/recurring-invoice-setup.html) Assign a recurring invoice to a party account.
